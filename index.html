Engineering Insights

  • Carnot Cycle provides the maximum theoretical efficiency for any heat engine operating between two temperatures.
  • Otto Cycle represents spark ignition (gasoline) engines — efficiency increases with compression ratio.
  • Diesel Cycle typically offers higher efficiency than Otto at equal compression ratios.
  • Rankine Cycle is the foundation of thermal steam power plants worldwide.

Engineering Recommendations

  • Increase compression ratio to improve Otto cycle efficiency.
  • Increase pressure ratio to improve Brayton cycle performance.
  • Improve condenser vacuum for better Rankine cycle efficiency.
  • Reduce thermal losses in heat exchangers with improved insulation.
